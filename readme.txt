Sharebear is a lightweight but powerful social sharing plugin for WordPress.

== Description ==
Add social sharing buttons to your posts, pages and custom post types.

= Sharing Buttons =
* Facebook
* Twitter
* Google+
* Pinterest
* Reddit
* LinkedIn
* email
* StumbleUpon

= Layouts =
Easily customize the share buttons to match the design of your website. Choose between 4 different layouts:

* Outlined
* Boxed
* Rounded
* None

= Styles & Sizes =
The social sharing icons can be integrated into your WordPress website in small, medium or large. You can display the buttons in gray or the respective brand colors.

= Locations =
Display your social sharing buttons as a floating sidebar, at the beginning and/or the end of your post, page and custom post type.

= Further Options =
* exclude specific posts/pages
* customize sharebear headline markup
* set your twitter handle
* customize email subject line & message
* bypass FontAwesome

== Installation ==
1. Download the sharebear.zip file to your computer.
1. Unzip the file.
1. Upload the `sharebear` folder to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the *Plugins* menu in WordPress.

To access the settings page navigate to *Settings > Sharebear* through your WordPress menu.
